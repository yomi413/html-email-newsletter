Seasons Greetings to one and all!

Visiting New York is fun, especially during the holidays. Thankfully, the city itself makes sure to put together its official guide of things to do in and around the city. Now, when I say the city I mean all 5 boroughs. Check out some of our favorite things to do in the Bronx, Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens and Staten Island. Which is your favorite?

Bronx
The Bronx is known for being home of the New York Yankees, the Bronx Zoo and the Botanical Gardens. As the only borough on the U.S. mainland not many people are willing to make the trip uptown to check out its appeal for many of its residents, including yours truly.

Holiday Train Show at the New York Botanical Garden

Running from November 23, 2019 - January 26, 2020, this show has become a staple of the Bronx and NYC as a whole.
 

Holiday Paint & Sip
Taking place on December 21, 2019, take in the sounds of Salsa, Jazz and R&B while your creativity shines through.
